Mini Mango Cheesecake Recipe!

Dawn Lee

Ingredients for this tasty dessert:

    For mango puree:
  • 150g of mango
  • 35g sugar
  • 5g lemon juice
    For fluffy cake layer:
  • 2 eggs
  • 60g sugar
  • 2g vanilla extract
  • 20g unsalted butter
  • 25g milk
  • 65g cake flour
    For cheesecake layer:
  • 20g water
  • 4g powdered gelatin
  • 200g cream cheese
  • 45g sugar
  • 3g vanilla extract
  • 5g lemon juice
  • 30g mango puree
  • 130g whipping cream

Now that we have all the ingredients prepared for this recipe, let's get started!

Steps to make this delicious mini mango cheesecake:

  1. Grab a ripe mango and cut it up into small pieces! You should have 150g of mango.
  2. In one pot, take your 150g of mango, 35g sugar, and 5g of lemon juice, and stir on medium heat until you have a smooth mango puree!
  3. Save 30g of your mango puree, this will be used later on!
  4. Grab a silicone circle mold tray of 3cm. Place your mango puree in a piping bag and fill up the mold.
  5. Place the mold in the freezer for about 2 hours.

Next, we're going to make our cake layer!

  1. In a bowl, mix 2 eggs, 60g sugar, and 2g vanilla extract together on low heat, until eggs become slightly translucent. Using a thermometer, this mixture should be around 40 degrees C.
  2. In a separate bowl, put 20g unsalted btter and 25g of milk together on low heat, but do not mix.
  3. With your egg mixture, mix until light and fluffly!
  4. In the same bowl that you just mixed, use a sifter to sift 65g of cake flour and mix with a spatula.
  5. Next, grab the bowl that had unsalted butter and milk---it should be melted now. And take one spoonful with the spatula in the butter and milk mixture, and mix together.
  6. Once mixed together, grab that mixture and pour it into your batter and mix with spatula. You should get a smooth batter!
  7. Pour into square cake tray of 21cm.
  8. Preheat your oven to 185 degrees.
  9. Bake in oven for about 22 minutes at 165 degrees C.
  10. Once baked, allow your cake to cool, then slice into 1.5m thick slices and cut into 5cm squares.

Now, let's make our cheesecake layer!

  1. In a small bowl, mix together 20g water and 4g of powdered gelatin for 10 minutes or more. Mix by shaking the bowl.
  2. In a large bowl, take your 200g of cream cheese, mix until soft.
  3. In the same bowl, add 45g sugar, 3g vanilla extract, and 5g of lemon juice. Whisk until smooth.
  4. From the mango puree you made earlier, add 30g to your bowl and continue whisking.
  5. Microwave your gelatin mixture for about 20 seconds, then add to your cheesecake mixture. Whisk until smooth.
  6. Grab 130g of heavy whipping cream and blend until it appears thick.
  7. Add your whipping cream in your cheesecake bowl and whisk until light.

Finally, let's assemble our cheesecake!

  1. Grab your mango puree from the silicone mold and attach it together to form a full circle.
  2. Then, grab your cake mold of 5cm, and with a piping bag, pour your cheesecake mixture a little above the halfway mark.
  3. Take your ball of mango puree and place it in the middle of the mold.
  4. Using a long tool like a chopstick or toothpick, slightly push the mango puree into the mixture until majority of the ball is submerged into the cheesecake.
  5. Grab your piping bag with cheesecake and cover the top of the mango puree.
  6. With your 5cm cut cake slices, place it at the top of the mold.
  7. Freeze your cake overnight.
  8. Once frozen, take out of mold and decorate with fresh mango pieces or anything of your choosing!
  9. Enjoy your delicious mini mango cheesecake!

And that's how you make a mini mango cheesecake, I hope you had fun baking with me!
